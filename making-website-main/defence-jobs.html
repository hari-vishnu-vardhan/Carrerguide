TechCareerGuide – Defense Jobs (Army, Navy, Airforce)

🔰 Job Role & Department

Defense jobs involve serving in the Indian Army, Navy, or Airforce, ensuring national security, border protection, and disaster relief operations.

📌 Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum qualification: 10+2 for NDA, graduation for CDS
  • Age limit: 16.5 to 19.5 years for NDA, 19 to 25 years for CDS
  • Medical fitness and physical standards are mandatory

💰 Salary Details

Starting salary: ₹40,000 to ₹80,000 per month depending on the rank and service branch.

📑 Exam & Application Details

  • Army: NDA, CDS, Technical Entry Scheme (TES)
  • Navy: NDA, SSR, Artificer Apprentice
  • Airforce: NDA, AFCAT, Airmen X & Y Group

🧠 Preparation Strategy

  • 📚 Books: Pathfinder for NDA, Arihant for CDS, Lucent GK
  • 📺 YouTube: Major Kalshi Classes, Study IQ, NDA CDS Exam Prep
  • 🧪 Mock Tests: NDA & CDS Test Series from Testbook, Adda247

📝 Exam Pattern

  • NDA: Mathematics (300 marks), General Ability Test (600 marks)
  • CDS: English (100), GK (100), Mathematics (100)

📄 Previous Papers & Tips

Solving previous years’ papers improves accuracy and understanding of actual questions. Focus on time management and physical training too.

🔗 Reference Websites

🎖️ Why Choose Defense Jobs?

Defense jobs offer prestige, adventure, and opportunities to serve the nation with great benefits, free accommodation, pension, and international training.
