Overview

Diploma in Printing Technology teaches the principles and practices involved in printing processes like offset, flexography, gravure, and digital printing. Students gain knowledge in prepress, press, and postpress operations along with computer graphics and quality control.

Subjects

  • Printing Processes
  • Print Media Technology
  • Offset and Digital Printing
  • Graphic Design and Layout
  • Packaging Technology
  • Print Machine Maintenance
  • Color Science & Management
  • Industrial Training & Project

Top Colleges

  • Government Institute of Printing Technology, Mumbai
  • Regional Institute of Printing Technology, Kolkata
  • AVPTI, Rajkot
  • Anna University (Printing Dept.), Chennai

Courses & Syllabus

The 3-year course offers theoretical and practical training in different printing methods. Emphasis is given to machine operation, printing inks, printing substrate, and color management techniques. Final year includes industrial training and a live project.

Career Opportunities

  • Prepress Technician
  • Digital Press Operator
  • Print Production Supervisor
  • Graphic Designer
  • Packaging Executive

Project Ideas

  • Color Accuracy Calibration System
  • Eco-friendly Printing Techniques
  • Automation in Packaging Line

Internships

Students can intern in newspaper agencies, publishing houses, packaging industries, and commercial printing companies. Companies like Times of India, Thomson Press, ITC Packaging, and Repro India Ltd offer internships.

Job Roles

  • Production Planner
