Overview

Diploma in Textile Engineering deals with the study of fabrics, fiber production, dyeing, finishing, and textile machinery. This course prepares students to work in textile manufacturing units, design industries, and research organizations focused on sustainable fabric technologies.

Subjects

  • Fiber Science & Yarn Manufacturing
  • Textile Testing & Quality Control
  • Fabric Structure and Weaving
  • Textile Dyeing and Printing
  • Garment Manufacturing
  • Polymer & Chemical Processing
  • Knitting Technology
  • Textile Machinery Maintenance

Top Colleges

  • Government Polytechnic, Nagpur
  • SVP Institute of Textile Management, Coimbatore
  • Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute, Mumbai
  • Govt. Polytechnic, Hisar

Courses & Syllabus

The course is divided across 6 semesters and covers the full life cycle of textile production, from raw fiber to finished garment. Workshops and labs on textile testing, machinery operation, and chemical treatments are included. Industry visits are regular throughout the curriculum.

Career Opportunities

  • Textile Engineer
  • Production Supervisor
  • Quality Control Executive
  • Dyeing Lab Technician
  • Textile Designer
  • Garment Merchandiser

Project Ideas

  • Eco-Friendly Natural Dye Project
  • Smart Fabric Integration
  • Low-cost Textile Testing Kit

Internships

Students can intern in textile mills, fashion export houses, and R&D labs. Companies like Raymond, Arvind Mills, Welspun, and NIFT-TEA provide hands-on training in production and design.

Job Roles

  • Weaving/Spinning Operator
  • Garment Quality Checker
  • Dyeing Technician
