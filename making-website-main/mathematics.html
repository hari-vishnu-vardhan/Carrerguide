Mathematics

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • Total Marks: 100
  • Written Exam: 80 marks
  • Internal Assessment: 20 marks
  • Sections:
    • Algebra & Polynomials - 15 marks
    • Geometry & Trigonometry - 25 marks
    • Mensuration - 10 marks
    • Statistics & Probability - 10 marks
    • Application-Based Problems - 20 marks

Detailed Syllabus

  • Algebra: Polynomials, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progression
  • Geometry: Triangles, Circles, Coordinate Geometry
  • Trigonometry: Trigonometric Ratios, Heights & Distances
  • Mensuration: Areas of Plane Figures, Surface Area & Volume
  • Statistics: Mean, Median, Mode, Probability

Reference Books

  • NCERT Mathematics (State Board Recommended)
  • RD Sharma (For Concept Clarity & Extra Practice)
  • RS Aggarwal (For Additional Examples & Solved Papers)

Websites for Study & Practice

  • Khan Academy
  • NCERT Official Website

Extra Tips

  • Practice previous years' question papers to understand exam patterns.
  • Understand formulas conceptually rather than memorizing them.
