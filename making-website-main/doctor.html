Doctor

TechCareerGuide

Overview: A doctor is a medical professional who diagnoses and treats illnesses, injuries, and other health conditions.

Responsibilities

  • Diagnosing patients' illnesses.
  • Prescribing treatments or medications.
  • Performing surgeries (specialized doctors).
  • Providing advice on lifestyle changes for improved health.

Available In

  • Private Sector: Private hospitals, clinics, and multinational healthcare companies.
  • Government Sector: Public hospitals, medical colleges, government healthcare organizations.

Educational Qualifications

  • M.B.B.S (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)
  • Postgraduate Degrees (MD, MS) for specialized fields.
  • Various medical certifications (optional).

Key Skills

  • Medical knowledge and expertise in diagnosis.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to remain calm under pressure.
