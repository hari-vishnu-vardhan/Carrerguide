Overview

Diploma in Automobile Engineering focuses on the design, production, and maintenance of vehicles. It combines mechanical, electrical, and electronics engineering with software systems to innovate and improve modern automobiles. This diploma equips students with skills in vehicle diagnostics, safety features, and emission technologies.

Subjects

  • Automobile Engine Technology
  • Chassis & Suspension Systems
  • Vehicle Transmission & Control
  • Automotive Electrical and Electronics
  • Fuel Systems & Emission Standards
  • Fluid Mechanics and Thermodynamics
  • Automobile Servicing & Diagnostics
  • CAD/CAE for Automotive Design

Courses & Syllabus

The diploma program is typically 3 years, divided into 6 semesters. Topics include IC engines, transmission systems, electric vehicles, automotive design using CAD tools, and safety & emission systems. Final semester involves industrial training and a capstone project.

Career Opportunities

  • Automotive Technician
  • Vehicle Test Engineer
  • Workshop Supervisor
  • Design Assistant in Automotive Sector
  • Quality Inspector in Auto Plants
  • Service Advisor at Showrooms

Project Ideas

  • Design of Hybrid Two-Wheeler
  • Anti-lock Braking System Model
  • Automatic Headlight System Based on Light Intensity

Internships

Students can intern at automobile companies like Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, Mahindra & Mahindra, Bosch, Hyundai, and Ashok Leyland. Internships focus on engine diagnostics, assembly line testing, and computer-aided vehicle inspections.

  • Vehicle Inspector
