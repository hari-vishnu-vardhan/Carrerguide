Teacher

TechCareerGuide

Overview: Teachers educate and inspire students in schools or universities, covering various subjects and specialties.

Responsibilities

  • Planning lessons and preparing course materials.
  • Teaching classes and providing individualized support.
  • Evaluating students' progress and providing feedback.
  • Participating in staff meetings and professional development activities.

Available In

  • Private Sector: Private schools, international schools, tutoring centers.
  • Government Sector: Government schools, universities, public education boards.

Educational Qualifications

  • B.Ed (Bachelor of Education) or equivalent.
  • Master's Degree (optional for higher education teaching).
  • Subject-specific qualifications or certifications (e.g., PhD for university-level teaching).

Key Skills

  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Patience and the ability to motivate students.
  • Strong subject knowledge and teaching methods.
