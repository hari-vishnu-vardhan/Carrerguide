Paramedical Jobs (Lab Technician, Pharmacist, ANM, GNM, Radiology Technician)

Job Role & Department

Paramedical jobs involve providing healthcare support services, assisting doctors, and handling medical diagnostics, laboratory tests, patient care, and emergency medical response.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Qualification: 10+2 with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Relevant diploma or degree (DMLT, BMLT, D.Pharm, GNM, ANM, Radiology Technician)
  • Age Limit: 18 to 35 years (varies based on position and department)

Salary Details

Basic salary: ₹25,000 - ₹60,000 per month depending on job role and experience.

Exam Details & Application Process

  • Exams conducted by State Health Departments, AIIMS, ESIC, and Railway Recruitment Board (RRB Paramedical)
  • Selection based on written exams and skill tests
  • Apply through government healthcare websites or recruitment boards

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: DMLT Guide, Pharmacology by KD Tripathi, Anatomy & Physiology
  • YouTube Channels: Medical Study Spot, Unacademy NEET
  • Mock Tests: Practice AIIMS, ESIC, and RRB paramedical papers

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • General Science (30 marks)
  • Biology & Human Anatomy (30 marks)
  • Medical Terminology & Patient Care (30 marks)
  • General Knowledge & Reasoning (10 marks)

Previous Year Questions & Additional Insights

Practicing previous years' papers helps in understanding important medical topics and question trends.

Reference Websites

  • ESIC Official Website
  • RRB Official Website

Extra Insights

Paramedical jobs offer a rewarding career in the healthcare sector with high job stability, government benefits, and opportunities in hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic labs.
