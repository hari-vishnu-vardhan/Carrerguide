Overview

D.Pharm is a two-year undergraduate diploma course in the field of pharmacy. It focuses on the fundamental aspects of pharmaceutical sciences, including the formulation, dispensing, and proper use of medications. The program prepares students for roles in community pharmacies, hospitals, and pharmaceutical companies.

Subjects

  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmacology & Toxicology
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Hospital & Clinical Pharmacy
  • Biochemistry & Clinical Pathology
  • Health Education & Community Pharmacy
  • Drug Store & Business Management

Internships

Internships are often undertaken at hospitals, retail pharmacies, and pharmaceutical companies to provide practical exposure in dispensing, compounding, and patient counseling.
