Diploma in Medical Lab Technology (DMLT)

← Back to Diploma Home

Overview

The DMLT course trains students in performing clinical tests and laboratory diagnostics to assist doctors in treating diseases. Medical lab technicians play a critical role in modern healthcare by handling blood, urine, stool, and tissue sample analysis.

Subjects

  • Clinical Biochemistry
  • Microbiology & Parasitology
  • Pathology & Hematology
  • Blood Banking & Transfusion
  • Histopathology Techniques
  • Biomedical Waste Management
  • Laboratory Equipment Maintenance

Internships

Students typically undergo 3 to 6-month internships in clinical laboratories, gaining hands-on experience in diagnostic procedures, lab safety protocols, and report generation.

Project Ideas
