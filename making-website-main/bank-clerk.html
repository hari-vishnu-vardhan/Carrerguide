Bank Clerk Jobs (IBPS & SBI Clerk)

Job Role & Department

Bank clerks work in public and private sector banks, handling customer transactions, maintaining records, and assisting with loan processing.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum educational qualification: 12th pass or graduation (varies by bank)
  • Age limit: 20 to 28 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Basic computer knowledge is required

Exam Details & Application Process

Recruitment is conducted through IBPS Clerk and SBI Clerk exams, which include prelims and mains.

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: Quantitative Aptitude by RS Aggarwal, English by Wren & Martin
  • YouTube Channels: Adda247, Unacademy
  • Mock Tests: Practicing previous year papers and taking online mock tests

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • Prelims: English, Numerical Ability, Reasoning (100 marks)
  • Mains: General Awareness, Computer Aptitude, Quantitative Aptitude (200 marks)
