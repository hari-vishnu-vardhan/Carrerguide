Overview

Diploma in Civil Engineering focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects such as buildings, roads, bridges, dams, and water supply systems. It combines technical, environmental, and managerial aspects of construction.

Subjects

  • Engineering Mechanics
  • Surveying
  • Building Construction
  • Concrete Technology
  • Structural Engineering
  • Hydraulics and Irrigation
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering

Top Colleges

  • Government Polytechnic, Mumbai
  • SBTET Colleges, Andhra Pradesh & Telangana
  • MSU Polytechnic, Vadodara
  • Aligarh Muslim University Polytechnic

Courses & Syllabus

The 3-year course includes theory and practicals in surveying, construction materials, RCC design, soil mechanics, and estimation. It also includes field visits, project work, and internships to strengthen real-world skills.

Career Opportunities

  • Site Engineer
  • Junior Civil Engineer
  • Surveyor
  • Construction Supervisor
  • Structural Draftsman

Project Ideas

  • Smart Drainage System Design
  • Low-Cost Housing Techniques
  • Rainwater Harvesting Project
  • Soil Stabilization Using Waste Materials

Internships

Students can intern at construction firms, municipal departments, railway projects, and infrastructure development boards like L&T, NCC, and CPWD. These offer exposure to field work and project management.

Job Roles
