Railway Jobs (Group C & D, RRB Clerk, Assistant, Ticket Collector)

Job Role & Department

Indian Railways recruits for various posts in Group C & D, including Clerk, Assistant, Ticket Collector, and technical roles.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum qualification: 10th pass for Group D, 12th pass or graduation for Group C
  • Age limit: 18 to 33 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Physical fitness required for certain posts

Salary Details

Average starting salary: ₹25,000 - ₹50,000 per month (varies by post)

Exam Details & Application Process

  • Exams conducted by RRB (Railway Recruitment Board)
  • Computer-based test (CBT), followed by physical and document verification
  • Apply through official RRB websites

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: R.S. Aggarwal for Reasoning, Lucent’s General Knowledge
  • YouTube Channels: Unacademy, Adda247
  • Mock Tests: Practicing previous year papers

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • General Awareness (40 marks)
  • Mathematics (30 marks)
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning (30 marks)

Previous Year Questions & Additional Insights

Practicing RRB past papers helps in understanding exam difficulty and question trends.

Reference Websites

  • RRB Official Website

Extra Insights

Railway jobs provide job security, government benefits, and promotions through departmental exams.
