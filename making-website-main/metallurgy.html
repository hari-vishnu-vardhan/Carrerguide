Overview

Diploma in Metallurgy focuses on the science and engineering of metals and alloys. It covers extraction, processing, heat treatment, and mechanical testing of metals used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.

Subjects

  • Engineering Materials
  • Metallurgical Thermodynamics
  • Physical Metallurgy
  • Heat Treatment of Metals
  • Mechanical Testing of Materials
  • Foundry Technology
  • Welding Technology
  • Powder Metallurgy

Top Colleges

  • Government Polytechnic, Nagpur
  • MSU Polytechnic, Vadodara
  • Government Polytechnic, Pune
  • Govt Polytechnic, Jhansi

Courses & Syllabus

The 3-year curriculum blends theory with lab work in metallurgy, alloy design, corrosion engineering, and testing techniques. It also includes CAD/CAM, project work, and industrial exposure during internships.

Career Opportunities

  • Quality Control Engineer
  • Metallurgical Technician
  • Heat Treatment Operator
  • Failure Analysis Engineer
  • Materials Engineer

Project Ideas

  • Corrosion Resistance Comparison in Metals
  • Powder Metallurgy Process Study
  • Optimization of Welding Parameters

Internships

Metallurgy students can intern in steel plants, foundries, automotive industries, and R&D labs. Companies like SAIL, Tata Steel, BHEL, and Mahindra offer internships for hands-on experience.

Job Roles

  • Metallurgist
  • Lab Technician
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Technician
  • Foundry Supervisor
  • Corrosion Engineer
