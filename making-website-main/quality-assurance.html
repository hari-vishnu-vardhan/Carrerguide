Quality Assurance

Quality Assurance professionals ensure that pharmaceutical products meet safety standards, regulatory guidelines, and specifications. They inspect, test, and document production processes.

  • Ensure compliance with Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP).
  • Test raw materials and finished products for quality standards.
  • Key skills: Analytical thinking, attention to detail, knowledge of regulations, communication skills.
