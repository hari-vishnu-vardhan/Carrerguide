Defense Jobs (Army, Navy, Airforce)

Job Role & Department

Defense jobs involve serving in the Indian Army, Navy, or Airforce, ensuring national security, border protection, and disaster relief operations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum qualification: 10+2 for NDA, graduation for CDS
  • Age limit: 16.5 to 19.5 years for NDA, 19 to 25 years for CDS
  • Medical fitness and physical standards are mandatory

Salary Details

Starting salary: ₹40,000 to ₹80,000 per month depending on the rank and service branch.

Exam Details & Application Process

  • Indian Army: NDA, CDS, Technical Entry Scheme
  • Indian Navy: NDA, SSR, Artificer Apprentice
  • Indian Airforce: NDA, AFCAT, Airmen X & Y Group

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: Pathfinder for NDA, Arihant for CDS, Lucent GK
  • YouTube Channels: Major Kalshi Classes, Study IQ
  • Mock Tests: Online test series for NDA, CDS, AFCAT

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • NDA: Mathematics (300 marks), General Ability (600 marks)
  • CDS: English (100 marks), GK (100 marks), Mathematics (100 marks)
