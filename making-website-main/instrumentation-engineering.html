Overview

Diploma in Instrumentation Engineering focuses on the design and maintenance of instruments and automation systems used in industrial processes. It includes the study of sensors, control systems, data acquisition, and instrumentation circuits, preparing students for careers in manufacturing, oil & gas, pharmaceuticals, and process industries.

Subjects

  • Electrical & Electronic Measurements
  • Process Control Systems
  • Industrial Instrumentation
  • Microcontroller Applications
  • Transducers & Sensors
  • Analytical Instrumentation
  • Digital & Linear Integrated Circuits
  • PLC & SCADA Systems

Courses & Syllabus

This 3-year diploma program offers a blend of electronics, control systems, and programming. Students get hands-on training in automation tools such as PLCs, SCADA, and LabVIEW. The final year includes industry training and a project based on automation or measurement systems.

Career Opportunities

  • Instrumentation Engineer
  • Control System Technician
  • Calibration Technician
  • Automation Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • SCADA Programmer

Project Ideas

  • Automatic Temperature Monitoring System
  • PLC Controlled Bottling Plant
  • IoT-Based Industrial Safety Alert System

Internships

Students can intern with companies in oil refineries, cement factories, chemical industries, and power plants. Organizations like BHEL, NTPC, L&T, and ABB offer internships in instrumentation and control systems.
