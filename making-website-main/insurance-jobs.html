Insurance Sector Jobs (LIC, UIIC, NICL Assistants)

Job Role & Department

Insurance jobs involve financial risk management, policy sales, and claim settlements. Roles include LIC Assistant, UIIC Assistant, and NICL Assistant.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum qualification: 12th pass for lower posts, Graduation for Assistant/Officer roles
  • Age limit: 18 to 30 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Basic knowledge of computers and banking is preferred

Salary Details

Basic salary: ₹25,000 - ₹50,000 per month (varies by role and experience)

Exam Details & Application Process

  • Conducted by LIC, NICL, UIIC, and other insurance companies
  • Exam Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview
  • Apply through the official websites of LIC, UIIC, NICL

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: Arihant Banking & Insurance, R.S. Aggarwal Quantitative Aptitude
  • YouTube Channels: Study IQ, Adda247, Testbook
  • Mock Tests: Regular practice with previous year question papers

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • Reasoning Ability & Numerical Ability (35 marks each)
  • General Awareness & English Language (30 marks each)
  • Computer Knowledge (30 marks)

Previous Year Questions & Additional Insights

Practicing previous year question papers helps understand the difficulty level.

  • UIIC Official Website
  • NICL Official Website
