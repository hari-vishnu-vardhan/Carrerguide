Overview

Diploma in Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) covers the study of electronic circuits, communication systems, embedded systems, and signal processing. It provides practical and theoretical knowledge essential for roles in electronics, telecom, embedded industries, and IoT sectors.

Subjects

  • Basic Electronics
  • Analog & Digital Electronics
  • Electronic Devices & Circuits
  • Microprocessors & Microcontrollers
  • Communication Systems
  • Embedded Systems
  • Industrial Electronics
  • Signal Processing

Top Colleges

  • Central Polytechnic College, Chennai
  • GP Hyderabad

Courses & Syllabus

The 3-year diploma course includes fundamentals of electronics, communication protocols, circuit design, embedded programming, and project work. Students gain hands-on experience in labs and industrial training.

Career Opportunities

  • Electronics Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Developer
  • Telecom Technician
  • PCB Design Engineer
  • Service & Maintenance Engineer

Project Ideas

  • Home Automation using Arduino
  • IoT-based Smart Irrigation
  • RFID Attendance System
  • Line Following Robot

Internships

Students can intern in electronics manufacturing units, telecom companies, R&D labs, and embedded system design firms. Top recruiters include BSNL, DRDO, BEL, and startups working in IoT.

Job Roles

  • Hardware Testing Engineer
  • VLSI Design Assistant
  • Telecom Operator
  • Embedded System Programmer
  • Signal Processing Analyst
