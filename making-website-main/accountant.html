Accountant

Accountants handle financial records, audits, and tax filings for companies or individuals. They help maintain compliance and advise on cost-saving measures.

  • Work in firms, government departments, or as consultants.
  • Prepare balance sheets, income statements, and budgets.
  • Key skills: MS Excel, Tally, GST compliance, tax filing, analytical thinking.
