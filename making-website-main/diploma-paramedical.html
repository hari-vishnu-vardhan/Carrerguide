Diploma in Paramedical Sciences

Overview

Paramedical Sciences include a variety of medical training programs that support diagnostic, therapeutic, and emergency services. These diploma courses equip students with practical skills and foundational knowledge needed in hospitals, clinics, and labs.

Major Diploma Courses under Paramedical Sciences

  • DMLT (Diploma in Medical Lab Technology)
  • Radiology & Imaging Technology
  • Operation Theatre Technology
  • Dialysis Technology

Core Subjects (Varies by Specialization)

  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Microbiology & Pathology
  • Biochemistry & Hematology
  • Medical Imaging Techniques
  • Operation Theatre Procedures
  • Pharmacology & Biomedical Instruments
  • Dialysis Operations & Patient Monitoring

Internship & Practical Training

Mandatory internships are provided at government and private hospitals where students assist in real-time patient diagnostics, operation setups, imaging, and dialysis. Training helps bridge the gap between classroom learning and practical application.

Industry Trends (2025+)

  • Wearable Health Monitors for Dialysis Patients
