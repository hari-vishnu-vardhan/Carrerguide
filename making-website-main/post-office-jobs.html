Post Office Jobs (PA, SA, MTS, Postman)

Job Role & Department

Post Office jobs include roles such as Postal Assistant (PA), Sorting Assistant (SA), Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS), and Postman. These jobs involve handling mail delivery, sorting, customer service, and clerical work.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Qualification: 10th or 12th pass (varies by post)
  • Age Limit: 18 to 32 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Basic computer knowledge is required for certain roles

Salary Details

Basic salary: ₹18,000 - ₹35,000 per month, plus allowances.

Exam Details & Application Process

  • Conducted by India Post under GDS (Gramin Dak Sevak) or SSC CHSL
  • Selection based on merit (10th/12th marks) or written exam
  • Apply through the official India Post website

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: Arihant Postal Exam Guide, Lucent General Knowledge
  • YouTube Channels: Testbook, Study IQ, Adda247
  • Mock Tests: Solve previous year question papers

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • General Knowledge (25 marks)
  • Mathematics (25 marks)
  • English Language (25 marks)
  • Reasoning Ability (25 marks)

Previous Year Questions & Additional Insights

Practicing previous year papers helps understand the pattern and important topics.

Reference Websites

  • India Post Official Website
  • SSC Official Website

Extra Insights

Post Office jobs provide job security, stable income, and benefits such as pension and medical insurance.
