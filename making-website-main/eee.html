Overview

Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE) offers foundational and advanced knowledge in electrical systems, machines, control systems, and electronics. It prepares students for roles in power generation, automation, instrumentation, and electrical maintenance.

Subjects

  • Electrical Circuits & Networks
  • DC & AC Machines
  • Power Electronics
  • Control Systems
  • Instrumentation & Measurement
  • Switchgear & Protection
  • Industrial Automation
  • Renewable Energy Technologies

Top Colleges

  • Government Polytechnic, Pune
  • PSG Polytechnic College, Coimbatore
  • Central Polytechnic College, Chennai
  • GP Vijayawada
  • Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Govt Polytechnic, Hyderabad

Courses & Syllabus

The diploma spans 3 years with subjects covering electrical theory, systems, machines, wiring, PLCs, and project work. Students also undergo lab sessions and industrial training in power plants and electrical workshops.

Career Opportunities

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Power Plant Technician
  • Substation Operator
  • PLC Programmer
  • Maintenance Engineer

Project Ideas

  • Automatic Street Light System

  • Home Automation with IoT

Internships

Internships are available in electricity boards, power plants, automation companies, and electrical equipment manufacturers like BHEL, NTPC, and L&T.
