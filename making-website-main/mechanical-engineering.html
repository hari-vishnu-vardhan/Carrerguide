Overview

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering focuses on the design, production, and operation of machinery. Students learn thermodynamics, manufacturing processes, CAD/CAM, and maintenance of mechanical systems. This diploma builds a strong foundation for industries such as manufacturing, automotive, energy, and aerospace.

Subjects

  • Engineering Mechanics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Strength of Materials
  • Machine Design
  • Manufacturing Technology
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Refrigeration & Air Conditioning
  • CAD/CAM

Courses & Syllabus

The 3-year program includes theoretical concepts and hands-on experience in workshops. Topics cover design software, kinematics, materials, and energy systems. Final year includes a major project and industrial training.

Internships

Students can intern in automotive companies, thermal plants, heavy machinery manufacturers, and tool design firms. Organizations like BHEL, L&T, Tata Motors, and Ashok Leyland offer internships to diploma holders.
