Police Jobs (Constable, Home Guard, Fireman, Forest Guard)

Job Role & Department

Police jobs involve law enforcement, maintaining public order, and preventing crime. Roles include Constable, Home Guard, Fireman, and Forest Guard.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum qualification: 10th/12th pass (varies by state & post)
  • Age limit: 18 to 27 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
  • Physical fitness is mandatory (running, height, chest measurements)

Salary Details

Basic salary: ₹21,700 - ₹69,100 per month (depending on post & state)

Exam Details & Application Process

  • Exams conducted by State Police Departments & SSC
  • Stages: Written Exam → Physical Test → Medical Test → Interview
  • Apply through the official state police websites

Preparation Strategy

  • Books: Lucent GK, R.S. Aggarwal Reasoning
  • YouTube Channels: Study IQ, Adda247
  • Mock Tests: Regular practice with previous year papers

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs (50 marks)
  • Mathematics & Reasoning (35 marks)
  • Language (15 marks)

Previous Year Questions & Additional Insights

Solving previous year question papers helps in understanding exam trends.

Reference Websites

  • Ministry of Home Affairs

Extra Insights

Police jobs offer respect, career growth, and opportunities to serve society.
