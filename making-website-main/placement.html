üìù 1. Resume Building (ATS Optimized)

  • üìòKeep it concise, action-oriented, ATS-friendly
  • Use reverse chronological order
  • Tailor it for each role with relevant keywords
  • One-page rule: Keep it concise (1 page for freshers, max 2 for experienced)
  • Action verbs: "Implemented", "Optimized", "Designed" instead of "Worked on"
  • Quantifiable achievements: "Improved performance by 40%" instead of "Made improvements"
  • Technical skills section: Group by proficiency (Advanced, Intermediate, Familiar)
  • Projects: Include 2-3 most relevant with GitHub links

üôã 7. HR Interview

  • Use STAR method
  • Be authentic and concise
üé• YouTube: Ankur Warikoo, CareerRide, Great Learning

Common Questions

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Your greatest strength/weakness
  • Why should we hire you?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Describe a challenging situation

STAR Method Examples

Situation: Final year, short-staffed coding team
Task: Fix bugs in a live app
Action: Used debugging tools, Git collaboration
Result: Fixed 11 bugs, 3 days ahead of schedule

Questions to Ask Interviewers

  • What does success look like in this role?
  • How does the team collaborate?
  • What are the current challenges?
  • Opportunities for professional growth?
