Overview

This diploma focuses on medical imaging techniques such as X-rays, CT scans, MRI, and ultrasound. It equips students to assist radiologists in diagnosing internal injuries and diseases through advanced imaging technologies.

Subjects

  • Radiographic Physics
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Radiographic Techniques
  • CT and MRI Imaging
  • Ultrasound Technology
  • Radiation Safety & Protection
  • Hospital Procedures & Ethics

Internships

Students usually complete clinical internships in hospitals, gaining experience in handling real-time imaging equipment, patient management, and diagnostic documentation.
