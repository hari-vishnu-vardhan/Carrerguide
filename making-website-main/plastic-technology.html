Overview

Diploma in Plastic Technology focuses on the processing, production, and design of plastic materials and products. It covers polymer science, plastic machinery, molding techniques, and recycling processes. Graduates play a key role in industries such as packaging, automotive, consumer goods, and medical devices.

Subjects

  • Polymer Chemistry
  • Plastic Processing Techniques
  • Mould Design & Manufacturing
  • Plastics Testing & Quality Control
  • Plastic Materials & Properties
  • Injection and Blow Molding
  • Extrusion Technology
  • Plastic Waste Management

Top Colleges

  • Central Institute of Plastics Engineering & Technology (CIPET), Chennai
  • Government Polytechnic, Mumbai
  • CIPET, Ahmedabad
  • Sri Jayachamarajendra Government Polytechnic, Bengaluru
  • CIPET, Bhubaneswar

Courses & Syllabus

This 3-year program includes study of thermoplastics, thermosetting plastics, polymer science, and processing equipment. The curriculum blends theory and practicals, and students undertake projects in product design, packaging technology, and plastic recycling.

Career Opportunities

  • Quality Control Technician
  • Process Engineer
  • Injection Molding Supervisor
  • Production Engineer
  • Polymer Technician

Project Ideas

  • Biodegradable Plastic Packaging
  • Plastic Waste to Fuel Conversion System
  • Design of Eco-Friendly Plastic Products

Internships

Internships are available in plastic manufacturing companies, packaging industries, and polymer research centers. Organizations like Reliance Industries, SRF Ltd., and CIPET offer hands-on experience with industrial plastic applications.
