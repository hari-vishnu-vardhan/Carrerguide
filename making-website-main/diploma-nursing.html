Overview

GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery) and ANM (Auxiliary Nurse Midwifery) are popular diploma courses in the nursing sector. These programs train students in patient care, midwifery, emergency response, and community health. They are essential for strengthening the primary healthcare workforce across the country.

Subjects

  • Fundamentals of Nursing
  • Community Health Nursing
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing
  • Psychology and Sociology
  • Pediatric Nursing
  • Mental Health Nursing
  • Midwifery and Obstetrical Nursing
  • First Aid & Nutrition

Internships

Internships are conducted at government and private hospitals, maternity clinics, and rural health centers. Students get practical experience in labor wards, operation theaters, and pediatric care units.
