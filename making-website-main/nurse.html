Nurse

Nurses provide critical care and support to patients in hospitals, clinics, and community settings.

  • Specialize in areas like pediatric, ICU, surgical, or public health nursing.
  • Assist doctors, monitor patient health, administer medications, and manage care plans.
  • In-demand globally, especially in both government and private health sectors.
