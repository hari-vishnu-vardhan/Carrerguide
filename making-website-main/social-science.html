Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)

Syllabus

  • History: Rise of Nationalism, World Wars, Indian Independence Movement, Industrial Revolution
  • Geography: Physical Features of India, Climate, Natural Resources, Population & Settlement
  • Civics (Political Science): Indian Constitution, Political Parties, Democracy & Diversity, Judiciary System
  • Economics: Money & Credit, Sectors of the Indian Economy, Globalization & Trade, Sustainable Development

Exam Pattern & Marks Distribution

  • Total Marks: 100
  • Written Exam: 80 marks
  • Internal Assessment: 20 marks (Assignments, Projects, Map Work)
  • Exam Sections: Multiple-choice questions, Short & Long Answer Questions, Map Work

Reference Books

  • NCERT Social Science Textbook
  • History - Spectrum’s "A Brief History of Modern India"
  • Geography - G.C. Leong’s "Physical Geography"
  • Civics - Laxmikant’s "Indian Polity" (For Basics)
  • Economics - Ramesh Singh’s "Indian Economy" (Basic Concepts)
