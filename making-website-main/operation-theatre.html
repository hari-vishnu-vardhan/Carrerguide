Overview

This diploma trains students to manage and assist in Operation Theatres, helping surgeons, nurses, and anesthetists during surgical procedures. It includes learning about sterilization, surgical instruments, anesthesia techniques, and emergency protocols.

Subjects

  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Medical Ethics & Terminology
  • Operation Theatre Techniques
  • Sterilization & Infection Control
  • Anesthesia Technology
  • Emergency & Trauma Care
  • Biomedical Equipment

Internships
