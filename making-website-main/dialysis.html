Overview

The Diploma in Dialysis Technology prepares students to operate dialysis machines, support kidney patients, and monitor their treatment. The course blends practical knowledge with theoretical understanding of kidney function and patient care.

Subjects

  • Pathology Related to Nephrology
  • Dialysis Technology
  • Biomedical Instrumentation
  • Pharmacology
  • Patient Monitoring
  • Clinical Dialysis Procedures

Internships

Internships are conducted in nephrology wards and dialysis centers, where students gain hands-on experience with hemodialysis equipment, patient management, and emergency handling procedures.
